## Company Profile

### Business Model

Tekion operates a **SaaS subscription model** billed primarily on a per-dealer/per-rooftop basis, with tiered pricing based on dealership size, module adoption, and third-party integrations. Revenue streams:

- **ARC subscriptions**: Recurring SaaS fees from franchised and independent dealers (DMS, CRM, F&I, parts, service modules)
- **AEC subscriptions**: Enterprise licensing from OEM manufacturers (GM, BMW, Ford, Honda, Toyota, Hyundai, Nissan-Renault-Mitsubishi)
- **APC marketplace**: Revenue-sharing from third-party technology partners using open APIs (grew 40x since launch)
- **Professional services**: Implementation, migration from CDK/Reynolds & Reynolds, training

The OEM investor base (GM, BMW, Nissan-Renault-Mitsubishi, Exor) serves as both capital and distribution — OEM-mandated DMS certifications accelerate dealer adoption across entire brand networks.

### Products

**ARC — Automotive Retail Cloud**: Full-stack, cloud-native DMS + CRM on a unified data model. Differentiators vs. CDK/Reynolds: unified data architecture (no ETL), mobile-first UX, AI-native agents (Service Advisor AI, Scheduler AI, Technician AI), open API-first (APC 2.0). 3,000+ features shipped in 2025.

**AEC — Automotive Enterprise Cloud**: For OEM headquarters — brand-wide dealer performance visibility, OEM-to-dealer communication, manufacturer order management and incentive integration. Used by 52 brands.

**APC — Automotive Partner Cloud**: Open API marketplace for the broader automotive tech ecosystem. APC 2.0 (Integration Hub) grew 40x since launch. Standard Open APIs provide near-real-time access to sales, service, inventory, and parts data.

Tekion handles some of the most sensitive data in automotive retail:
- **PII**: Customer names, SSNs, addresses, credit application data

- **AI/ML**: Unstructured voice + video (Technician AI), scheduling behavioral data, inference logs

Compliance stack: GLBA / FTC Safeguards Rule, CCPA/CPRA, GDPR (EU expansion), SOC1 + SOC2 Type II, ISO 27001:2022, ISO 27701:2019 (privacy), ISO 42001:2023 (AI governance). Published DPA for GDPR/CCPA alignment.
